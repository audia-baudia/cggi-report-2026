Section Four

Where We're Going

Our priorities for the year ahead, organized around growth, experience, and resources.

Goal One

Add Programs

Keep building the footprint. Every new program is more athletes, more fans, and more proof that this sport belongs on every campus.

What This Looks Like

Active Pipeline. Real Conversations.

Building on momentum from McMurray and the ongoing Texas State follow-up, we'll keep working a clear pipeline of prospective programs and supporting them through launch.

Goal Two

Enhance The Fan Experience

Two fronts. Both matter.

Postseason

Championships

Build on the Fan Fest blueprint. Make the Championships environment unmistakably premier, year after year.

Every Weekend

Regular Season

Bring the same fan-first thinking to regular-season meets. The product is great; the experience around it should match.

Goal Three

Grow Every Program

A stronger sport means stronger programs at every level. We'll lean into education and direct support.

  • Monthly educational Zooms. Sessions led by our own members, sharing what's working at their programs.
  • Direct support. Programmatic help available to any program in the association that needs it.
  • Members teaching members. The expertise is already in the room, we just need to make it easier to share.
Goal Four

Fund The Future

We keep looking for funding sources, and we go back to an idea that has worked before.

The Idea

Camp Donations: $ Per Camp

A simple, repeatable model. A flat per-camp donation from member programs creates a steady, predictable funding base while we continue to pursue larger sources of support.

Predictable. Member-powered. Easy to start.

Add Programs. Elevate The Experience. Grow The Sport.

That's the assignment. That's the year ahead.
